About Christ Embassy

Christ Embassy, also known as Believers’ Loveworld, is a global ministry founded by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome. The ministry is committed to taking the divine presence of God to the nations of the world through teaching the Word of God and demonstrating the power of the Holy Spirit.

Christ Embassy Kempton Park is part of this global vision, focused on building faith, raising leaders, and transforming lives through prayer, worship, and sound biblical teaching.
